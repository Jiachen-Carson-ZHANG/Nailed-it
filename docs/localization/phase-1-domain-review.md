# Phase 1 Domain Review

First-wave domain review covers the catalog and seeded style naming used by `booking`, `style`, and `manage`. Audit for terminology accuracy, service-industry naturalness, and consistency between catalog labels and seeded style names.

## Core Service Modules

| ID | Type | zh-CN | en | Review Focus |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| basic_manicure_service | catalog_item | 基础护理服务 | Basic manicure service | Confirm whether "manicure service" is the right umbrella term for prep, base gel, and shaping. |
| removal_service | catalog_item | 卸甲服务 | Removal service | Check whether "removal" alone is clear enough for salon operators. |
| builder_service | catalog_item | 建构服务 | Builder gel service | Verify that "builder gel" matches merchant vocabulary better than plain "builder". |
| extension_service | catalog_item | 延长服务 | Nail extension service | Keep aligned with merchant pricing expectations. |
